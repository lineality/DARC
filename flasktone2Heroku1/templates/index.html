D.A.R.C.

Distributed Asynchronous RGP Coordinator

Project DARC is a modular and mod-able platform that should allow groups of people to carry out a traditional table-top RPG campaign, despite having players and a DM (the person running the game) in different places (distributed) and not all online at the same time (asynchronous).

DARC is designed to be simple, secure, portable (mobile), and flexible/customizable.
